The Roads
Spiti roads are not roads — they're suggestions. Loose gravel, blind corners, single-lane paths cut into cliffsides. Every kilometre earns your respect.
Earned every metre
🌌
Nights in the Valley
No light pollution, no sound. Spiti at night has the kind of sky that makes you feel small in the best possible way. Stars so dense it looks like someone spilled milk across the sky.
Best skies I've seen
🤝
The People
In the most remote villages, complete strangers offered food, shelter and help without being asked. That generosity — no transaction, no reason — stays with you longer than any photo.
Changed my perspective
